Mirada

Mirada is an advanced image management and retrieval system. Designed for teams handling high volumes of image archives. This is the fruit of a collaboration with our Italian partner: Nowhere.

eHealth

Meet Infodoctor.org, our eHealth Spanish information service, where we leverage 15 years of work as the pioneer of health care information on the Internet since 1999. Don't miss our book where we explain our vision of the data-driven revolution of healthcare, based on our own experience in the field as consultants.

Click ARM

ImasD Tecnologia is the most audacious Spanish mobile devices manufacturer. They have created the first modular tablet: Click ARM. Partnering with Opentix, we developed the web application to pre-order Click ARM tablets with custom configuration.
